About Me

I am from Kumamoto Japan and have lived in the California Bay Area for more than 10 years. I began teaching Japanese lessons in 2017 and I love what I do!

I have many passions: I love languages, reading, music, dance, animals, art, traveling, and all the beautiful things in the world! Especially "Tabemono" (Japanese word for food).


Private / Small Group Lessons

I teach all levels and all ages. We can meet online or in-person (or a combination of both is ok!).

I have many Japanese text books and materials including Manga/Anime to use, but all it's up to you! I can train you for JLPT, conversation practice, translations, etc... A personalized lesson for each! Please let me know what you would like to focus on and what your goals are.
